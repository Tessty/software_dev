General Data Analyst Salary Expectations
Entry-Level: Around $80,000 to $100,000 per year.
Mid-Level: $90,000 to $130,000 per year.
Senior-Level: $120,000+ per year, potentially reaching $200,000 or more in high-paying industries or locations.
Factors Influencing Salary
Experience: More experience generally translates to higher earning potential.
Skills: Strong coding skills, advanced analytical abilities, and domain expertise can command higher salaries.
Location: Salaries vary by region, with major metropolitan areas and tech hubs often offering higher pay.
Industry: Certain industries, like finance and big tech, tend to pay more for data analytics roles.
Role: Specialized roles like data scientist or analytics manager can command higher salaries than entry-level data analyst positions.
Specific Programs and Companies
Google Data Analytics Certificate: ZipRecruiter reports the average salary for those with the Google Data Analytics Certificate is around $81,518 per year.
Coursera: Data analysts with Coursera certifications can expect salaries ranging from $80,000 to $112,000 per year.
IBM Data Analyst Professional Certificate: A median entry-level salary of $95,616.

Google Salaries: An average of $150k.
